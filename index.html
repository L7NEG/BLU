FAQ

How to use?

First, download the dll from the above download button, then download the key for the menu from the Download Your Key section. After that run the game, load the dll in the injector and inject the dll in-game. A black bar will come up to type the key, enter your key there and press enter. The menu is now ready to use and will pop up on pressing F4.

Does the menu work in GTA Online?

Yes, the menu works in both the Single Player and the Multi-Player game modes.

Is the Menu safe?

The menu is currently undetected and completely safe to use.
